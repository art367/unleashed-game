## 🎮 The Story

This game was born from a single idea — *"What if you were a dog that just slipped off the lead?"* — and built entirely by AI in one conversation. No frameworks, no build tools, no second chances. Just pure, joyful chaos coded in real-time.

Inspired by [that legendary Fenton video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3GRSbr0EYYU), **UNLEASHED!** puts you in the paws of a freshly-freed dog, sprinting through a park chasing squirrels, ducks, deer, and other dogs while your owner loses their mind behind you.

The owner's voice? That's your browser's speech synthesis, screaming your dog's name with increasing desperation. You're welcome.

---

## 🕹️ How to Play

**Move** your dog with arrow keys, mouse, or touch. **Dash** with spacebar, click, or double-tap. Chase animals. Grab power-ups. Avoid obstacles. Don't get caught.

That's it. You don't need a tutorial. You're a dog. *Act like one.*

## 🐾 What's Inside

- **3 Breeds** — Labrador (balanced), Greyhound (speed+), Terrier (agility+)
- **4 Animal Types** — Squirrels (zig-zag), Ducks (waddle), Deer (big score, flee AI), Other Dogs (chaos)
- **Owner Panic System** — Voice lines escalate from *"Here boy!"* to *"OH JESUS CHRIST!"* using Web Speech API
- **Dynamic Difficulty** — Speed ramps, obstacles multiply, owner closes in
- **Power-ups** — 🎾 Tennis balls (speed boost), 🦴 Bones (score multiplier), 🍗 Food (pushes owner back)
- **Combo System** — Chain catches for multiplied points
- **Chaos Ratings** — From *Good Boy 😇* to *ABSOLUTE LEGEND 👑*
- **Juice** — Particles, screen shake, floating text, glowing pickups, animated everything
- **1,600 lines** of hand-crafted vanilla JS, zero dependencies, one file
